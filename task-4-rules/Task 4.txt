Part B – Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Here’s a simple checklist you can save as social-media-checklist.md or .txt:
✅ Do’s
- Do maintain a professional profile picture and bio.
- Do verify information before sharing or reposting.
- Do respect privacy by adjusting account settings appropriately.
- Do engage positively and constructively in discussions.
- Do credit sources when sharing academic or creative content.
❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal information publicly.
- Don’t post offensive or discriminatory content.
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative trolling.
- Don’t plagiarize or copy content without permission.
- Don’t spend excessive time online at the cost of academics.
